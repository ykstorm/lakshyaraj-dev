# Guardrail Proxy

Advanced safety guardrails for LLM applications with streaming support.

## Safety Features

- **Hard-Abort**: Immediate stop on policy violation
- **Soft-Observe**: Log and monitor without blocking
- **Streaming**: Real-time streaming compatible
- **Flexible**: Mix and match guardrail modes

## Design

Implements multi-modal safety strategies for different risk profiles and use cases.
