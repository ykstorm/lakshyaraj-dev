# Building Resilient Systems

Resilience is built into systems through careful design and defensive programming.

## Core Principles

- Graceful degradation
- Fallback mechanisms
- Circuit breakers
- Proper error handling

## Implementation

Apply these patterns consistently across your service layers to build systems that degrade gracefully under failure.
